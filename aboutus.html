READING PROGRAM

Core Reading is a comprehension and vocabulary based program, designed for students in grades two to twelve who need fundamentals as well as those wanting a competitive edge. Daily Reading Worksheets will take about 30 minutes to complete – in two sessions a week and another five assignments completed at home. Your child will work at his or her own pace, mastering each new reading concept before moving on. With each worksheet, your child’s overall reading and writing skills will advance incrementally. Advance your child’s reading skills from an early age, and throughout their school. Building strong reading comprehension from an early age will prepare your child to advance through elementary school with confidence and appreciation for learning. Through the study of advanced fiction and non-fiction passages, your child will develop critical thinking skills by analyzing complex texts from literary criticism to academic, social, and political commentary.

Helping Your Child Learn to Read

There is probably no more important activity for preparing your child to succeed as a reader than reading aloud together. Fill your story times with a variety of books. Be consistent, be patient, and watch the magic work. Even after children learn to read by themselves, it’s still important for you to read aloud together. By reading stories that are on their interest level, but beyond their reading level, you can stretch young readers’ understanding and motivate them to improve their skills. We aim to engage young minds and make learning more engaging — but we also appreciate that the end result should be better academic results.
